Origin

From the love to Vietnam and the passion of coffee, in 1999, the brand named Lowland Coffee was born with the passion of raising the café heritage of Vietnam and spreading the spirit of Viet pride, connecting harmoniously between tradition and modernity. Originating as a packaged coffee business in Hanoi in 2000, we’ve quickly grown into a chain of well-known coffee shops that have been expanding throughout Vietnam and abroad since 2002. Over the time we’ve become famous for consistently delivering delicious Vietnamese coffee in a modern and comfortable environment that reflects the appeal of modern Vietnamese life. Today we continue to hand select the finest beans that are roasted fresh daily and served with a smile. Great Cafe - Great Product - Great Service at an affordable price is the secret of our success!
